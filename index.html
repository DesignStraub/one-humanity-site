About

Somewhere between millions of people, we still found each other.

One Planet, One Humanity is a small independent project about remembering something simple: we are not separate from each other. We share this planet, this moment and the same need to feel seen.

The stickers, art pieces and tools are made as small signals of connection — on a laptop, in a room, on the street or in the hands of a stranger who needed a softer moment.

No perfect philosophy. No brand pretending to save the world. Just a growing collection of human-made reminders: you belong here, and so does everyone else.
